About Us

ACP Entertainment was founded in 2008 as mainly an

inflatable rental company run out of a garage in Byron

Center. Over the years, ACP has grown to be one of the

largest entertainment companies in the State of Michigan

servicing between 500-700 events each year including

company picnics, school events, corporate events, college

campus events, Christmas parties, senior all nighters, post

proms, festivals and private events. Our experienced staff is

eager to assist you in your next event!

"Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as

working for the Lord, not for men.”-Colossians 3:23

MEET OUR LEADERSHIP TEAM

ANDREW SUNDSTROM

General Manager

Andrew has been with ACP Entertainment since Fall of 2018 but is not new to

the entertainment industry. He previously managed a family fun center for

12 years prior to working for ACP Entertainment. He is responsible for

everything operations here at ACP Entertainment including scheduling,

routing, repair, maintenance and improvement projects. On his spare time,

Andrew loves to spend time with his 2 kids and wife, play softball and also

sells real estate on the side. He's a busy guy!

RICK STRAYHORN

Operations Manager

Rick has been with ACP Entertainment since day one. He is the father of

Ryan (ACP's founder) and has always been heavily involved. As the

company grew and offered new services, Rick then moved on to manage

Dad’s Classic Catering, a previous division of ACP Entertainment. Now he is

the operations manager at ACP Entertainment with the role of routing,

scheduling and team member relations. Rick is now a retired teacher of

over 15 years. On his spare time he enjoys camping and spending time with

his family. Rick has the best spirit and attitude for helping and serving

others!

KRISTIN VANDERMEER

Event Planning Specialist

Kristin joined the team in January of 2022 and is typically the first team

member you'll speak with when calling in! Kristin is responsible for

customer relations, planning events and human resources for our team.

Kristin worked as a crew member at ACP Entertainment many years ago

and was excited to come back full time working in the office. In her spare

time, Kristin loves to spend time with her two kids and family along with

spending time outdoors.

RYAN STRAYHORN

Founder and President

Ryan Started ACP Entertainment in 2008 as a DJ, balloon twister and with a

few inflatables. Now his main roles are planning large events and oversee

all operations. Since he founded the company, Ryan has an extreme

passion for bringing new ideas to the table, innovating and continuing to

advance ACP Entertainment in the entertainment industry. Ryan is

definitely a bit of a workaholic, however he does take a break once in a

while! In his free time he enjoys traveling with his Wife, camping and is also

quite the Disney Parks buff. In fact, Ryan was trained by the Disney Institute

which is a professional development course offered by the Walt Disney

Company.
