Carnival Ride Rental

Make your next event a carnival! Choose from a wide

selection of carnival rides that include attractions for all

ages. Most rides include an LED lighting package so your

events will be visible during the day and night. Plus, our

trained operators set up and operate each ride so you can

focus on having fun and making memories.

OUR CARNIVAL RIDES FOR RENT

If you’re looking for carnival rides for birthday parties, graduation

celebrations, or any other type of event that calls for backyard

carnival rides, look no further. We offer all types of rides for any age,

anywhere in Michigan. From the traditional to the unexpected, we

have a ride that’ll make any event memorable.
