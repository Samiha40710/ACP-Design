DESCRIPTION

The Ballistic Swing carnival ride is a thrilling fixed chair

swing that will make you feel as if you’re soaring through

the air since you’re almost horizontal with the ground! The

Ballistic Swing carnival ride is a thrilling attraction for all

ages and can fit up to 16 riders at once. This swing ride also

brightly illuminates at night with bright and vibrant color

changing LED’s. Bring the Ballistic swing carnival ride to

your next company picnic, festival, school event or

community event and wow your guests!

Due to it’s popular demand, in 2020 ACP Entertainment

released an all new Ballistic, what we are calling Ballistic

2.0. This completely new swing has an all new paint color

scheme and even more lights that are almost two times

as bright asthe first generation!
